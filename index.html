Why Choose Us?

Find help with your bookings and ticketing shows, and see what to expect along your journey.

01

Secure Payment

We prioritize the security of your transactions. Choose from our various payment options, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or other secure payment gateways. Rest assured, your payment information is encrypted and protected at all times.

02

Convenience

Say goodbye to long queues and last-minute rushes. With just a few clicks, you can book your tickets anytime, anywhere, from the comfort of your home or on the go.

03

Confirmation & E-Ticket

After successful payment, you'll receive an instant confirmation via email or SMS, along with your e-ticket or booking voucher. Simply present this e-ticket or voucher at the venue or during boarding, and you're all set!

frequently Asked Questions?

Booking Process:

You can book tickets online through our Website, Whatsapp or by visiting our box office in person during opening hours.

Can i reserve tickets without purchasing them immediately:

Unfortunately, we do not offer reservation. Tickets are sold on a first-come, first-served basis.

What payment methods do we accept:

We accept payment by credit/debit card, USSD, and select other payment methods. Cash payment are accepted at our box office.

Can i get a refund if i can no longer attend the show:

Refund policies vary dependingon the show and ticket type. please refer to our refund policy or contact us for assistance
